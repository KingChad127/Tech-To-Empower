Tech Donations

If you have an old laptop, tablet, or phone and aren't sure what to do with it, consider donating it to Tech to Empower. These donations are useful to people in the DFW metropolitan area who lack access to basic technology. Tech Donations are then checked to ensure that they are in full working condition and properly reset to ensure that the donor's information is no longer on the device. If we receive a tech donation that is not in adequate condition, we may either repair/refurbish it, or if the cost to do so is considerable, then we will work with e-waste partners to ensure that the device is properly recycled.
