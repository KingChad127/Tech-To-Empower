Donations

How to Donate

Tech to Empower takes two different types of donations. You can assist by donating money through GoFundMe or by donating used technology such as laptops, desktops, phones, and tablets.

How We Use Donations

Once the GoFundMe goal has been reached we use the donations to order chromebooks in bulk. These chromebooks and other direct donations will then be distributed to the organization that we partner up with to ensure that the technology is used to help people who need tech to improve their education, job outlook, etc.
