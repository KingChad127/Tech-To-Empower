About Us

Tech to Empower is a non-profit organization founded by three seniors from Flower Mound High School. We collect donations of old technology, and use monetary donations to purchase used chromebooks. We then distribute these donations to local causes that benefit from tech donations.

How to Donate

Tech to Empower currently accepts physical tech donations such as laptops, tablets, phones, and prepaid internet devices. We are looking to add online monetary donations through GoFundMe in the future. If you would like to contribute monetary donations contact us and we can accept in person checks and cash donations.

Donate Today

How We Use Donations

Once the GoFundMe goal has been reached we use the donations to order chromebooks in bulk. These chromebooks and other direct donations will then be distributed to a local organization that we partner up with, such as a homeless shelter, soup kitchen, or children's home, to ensure that the technology is used to help people who need tech to improve their education, job outlook, etc.
