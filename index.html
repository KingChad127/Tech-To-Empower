About Us

Tech to Empower is a non-profit organization founded by three seniors from Flower Mound High School. We collect donations of old technology, and use monetary donations to purchase used chromebooks. We then distribute these donations to local causes that benefit from tech donations.

How to Donate

Tech to Empower accepts physical tech donations such as laptops, tablets, phones, and prepaid internet devices. If you have an old laptop, tablet, or phone and aren't sure what to do with it, consider donating it to Tech to Empower. Depending on your location, we will either come collect your donation, or work out a halfway location where we can collect it. We also accept monetary donations online through Paypal. If you'd like to donate through cash or check, please contact us.

How We Use Donations

When we receive tech donations, we check that they are in full working condition and properly reset to ensure that the donor's information is no longer on the device. If we receive a tech donation that is not in adequate condition, we may either repair/refurbish it, or if the cost to do so is considerable, then we will work with e-waste partners to ensure that the device is properly recycled.

Monetary donations are used to purchase used chromebooks in bulk. Monetary donations are also used to fund repairs and refurbish tech donations that we have received. Tech donations that were already in good condition, refurbished tech donations, and purchased chromebooks are then given to people who would benefit from access to technology. We work with distribution partners such as soup kitchens, childrens homes, and homeless shelters to ensure that technology reaches the people who need it the most.
